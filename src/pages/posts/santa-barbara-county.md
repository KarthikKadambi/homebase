Santa Barbara County offers a unique cultural escape. Tucked away in the Santa Ynez Valley is a town that feels like a fairy tale come to life.

### Highlights of the Santa Barbara County

#### Solvang
- **Solvang**: Known as the "Danish Capital of America," this town features traditional Danish architecture, windmills, bakeries offering aebleskiver, and a charming European atmosphere.
