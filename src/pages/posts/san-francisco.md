San Francisco captivates with its rolling hills, Victorian architecture, and stunning bay views. It's a city that feels both historic and forward-looking, with a charm that is uniquely its own.

## Highlights of the Trip

### Waterfront & Classics
- **Fisherman's Wharf & Pier 39**: The bustling heart of tourism, complete with sea lions and clam chowder.
- **Ferry Building**: A foodie's paradise with great views of the Bay Bridge.
- **Ghirardelli Square**: A sweet stop for legendary chocolate.
- **Palace of Fine Arts**: A monumental structure that feels like stepping into a painting.

### City Views & Streets
- **Coit Tower**: Standing tall on Telegraph Hill with panoramic views.
- **Columbus Tower (Sentinel Building)**: A stunning flatiron building in North Beach.
- **Lombard Street**: The famous "crookedest street in the world."
- **Cable Car**: Riding the historic rails up and down the steep hills.
- **Twin Peaks**: The ultimate viewpoint to see the entire city spread out below.
